# LoanSync

**Simple & Smart Collaborative Loan Management for Groups & Families**

## Overview

In India, many people take group loans (like *DWACRA* in Andhra Pradesh), family loans, or business partner loans. But managing these shared loans is messy — tracking who paid what, verifying payments, calculating individual liability, handling early or missed payments, and dividing interest fairly becomes a big headache.

**LoanSync** is a mobile-first web application that makes group loan management transparent, fair, and stress-free. It helps friends, families, and business partners track payments, projections, and liabilities in real-time with clear accountability.

## Features

- **Create Shared Loans**  
  Easily create group loans (Personal, Family, Business, Gold Loan, etc.) and add participants. The creator becomes the admin.

- **Flexible Liability Sharing**  
  Split loan liability equally or in custom ratios (e.g., 40%-30%-30%).

- **Dynamic Projection Engine**  
  Get a clear monthly repayment schedule for every participant — showing EMI, interest, principal, and remaining balance. Projections update automatically when payments are made early, late, or partially.

- **Payment Recording & Verification**  
  Participants can record payments with date, amount, and receipt/proof (image or PDF). Other members can view and verify the transaction.

- **Admin Controls**  
  Only admins can edit loan details and liabilities. Participants can only view their own data and projections.

- **Clean Dashboard**  
  Overview of total outstanding, next due, active loans, and individual loan progress.

- **Ledger & Transaction History**  
  Complete transparent ledger with payment status (Verified, Pending, Rejected).
